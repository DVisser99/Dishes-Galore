How to Wash Your Dishes

Section 1: Scrape

Remove as much food residue as possible by scraping plates and bowls with a utensil, dumping the contents into your trash bin.

Section 2: Soak

Set your faucet to hot and soak your plates and bowls in the hot water. Place your utensils on top of your plates and bowls. Soaking your dishes and silverware in hot water will loosen any remaining food residue. Let your dishes soak for 30 minutes.

Section 3: Scrub

Apply a small amount of dish soap onto your brush or sponge and begin to wash each dish. Vigorously work away remaining food residue. Be sure to wash the front and back sides for a complete clean. Scrub each dish until clean.

Section 4: Dry

After washing, place each dish in a drying rack while cleaning other dishes. Once all of your dishes are clean, take a dry towel and wipe your dishes until dry.
